Data

The aim of the Elections NI is not only to produce easily accessible information, but also to make it available to others to use, reuse and share.

We use a structured database framework for storing candidate, constituency and results information. Representing the Assembly elections Single Transferable Vote (STV) counting system isn't straighforward, but we think the layout shared with us by the Irish Local Government Management Agency (LGMA) works well.

Our datasets contain information from the Electoral Office for Northern Ireland (made available under the Open Government Licence)

At the moment we are focusing on the Assembly Elections. We'll be looking at past Assembly, Westminster and Local Government elections, but as this is a time-intensive process, for the meantime we recommend the ARK 'CAIN' webservice digitisation of past elections and referenda.
