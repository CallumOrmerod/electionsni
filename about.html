The Project

The Elections NI Open Data project is a collaboration between a number of people who came together to produce datasets and visualisations of the 2016 Northern Ireland Assembly elections.

We don't receive funding for the project and we are independent of any political party or candidate. However, we are grateful to NICVA for providing the domain name.
